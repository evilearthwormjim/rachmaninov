Sorcha Barry Piano School
Classes

Piano lessons are available Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday:

  • Classes are booked on a term basis. Fees must be paid in full at the beginning of each term.
  • Performing is highly encouraged! We have a Hot Chocolate Party every Christmas and a Summer Recital at the end of the school year. This is a great opportunity for our students to meet other pianists and inspire or be inspired by their peers.
  • Practice is absolutely crucial when learning an instrument. The key to enjoyment and progress is consistent practice. Students are given a practice journal that they must fill out each week. This helps them and their teacher to keep a record of all their work and highlight what areas need improvement. Students who practice a little every day improve much quicker than those who just do one panic practice session before their lesson! As Suzuki said; “Knowledge is not skill. Knowledge plus 10,000 times is skill.”

Lessons will be conducted on a Yamaha CLP-470 Piano which features:

  • Solid natural wooden keys, just like a grand piano
  • Faithfully reproduces the differences in the weight and return of each key (88- Key Linear Graded Hammers)
  • Pedal that provides the same feel as a grand piano
  • New generation piano sound source provides outstanding playing response and expressive capability
