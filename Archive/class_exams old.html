Sorcha Barry Piano School
Exams

As the Galway representative for the London College of Music examinations, Sorcha believes that working with their syllabus achieves extremely high and professional standards.
Although it is encouraged to aim for an exam every year, the choice is entirely up to the student and parent. She teaches the syllabus in a fun and non-stressful way, allowing the student to be able to play a wide variety of repertoire while encouraging a creative, musical process.

LCM examination accreditation is recognised internationally.
You can check out the London College of Music examinations website here:
www.uwl.ac.uk/lcmexams
