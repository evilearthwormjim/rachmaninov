Sorcha Barry Piano School
Biography

Sorcha Barry - BMUS, ALCM, DipLCM, ADPA, RAD Ballet Teacher, CBTS:RAD,

  • Bachelor of Music (Hons) majoring in Performance, NUI, Maynooth
  • Diploma in Piano Performance (distinction), London College of Music
  • Associate Diploma of Performing Arts, Performance Arts Awards
  • Associate of the London College of Music (ALCM)
  • Certificate in Ballet Teaching Studies (RAD)

Sorcha is a classically trained pianist, with an honours degree in Music, majoring in performance from the University of Ireland, Maynooth.
She has completed all grades in the piano and holds an Associate Diploma and a Diploma in Piano Performance with the London College of Music. In 2012 she achieved her ADPA; an internationally recognised diploma qualification developed for teachers working with young people in the performing arts.
Sorcha taught Piano and Keyboard for two years in Ratoath School of Music and Glasnevin School of Music before moving back to Galway and setting up her own school. The school began in 2012 in Lower Salthill before moving to Cooke's Corner in 2016 and finally finding a permanent home with fully dedicated piano studios on Shantalla Road in 2018.

Sorcha has played piano professionally for functions, fundraisers, weddings and ceremonies.

Sorcha is also a registered ballet teacher with the Royal Academy of Dance. She graduated from the Academy in 2015 with an International Certificate in Ballet Teaching Studies. Sorcha completed Grade 8 RAD Ballet, Intermediate, Advanced Foundation and Advanced 1 Vocational level in ballet examinations.
