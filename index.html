Welcome!

The Sorcha Barry Piano School provides students with the skills and appreciation of piano to become proficient musicians. Through weekly tuition, consistent practice and recitals, the piano school encourages a love of music and performance. We pride ourselves in teaching in a safe and nurturing environment where students feel happy and comfortable.

Our dedicated piano studio is in Galway city within walking distance of NUIG.
