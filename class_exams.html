Sorcha Barry Piano School
Exams

Sorcha is the Galway representative for the London College of Music examinations. She believes that working with their syllabus achieves an extremely high standard. The syllabus includes very diverse pieces of music which candidates love to learn and play.

Exams are a good focus for students. Although we encourage students to do piano exams, the choice is up to the student and parent. The syllabus allows the student to be able to play a wide variety of repertoire while encouraging a creative, musical process.

LCM examination accreditation is recognised internationally.
You can check out the London College of Music examinations website here:
www.uwl.ac.uk/lcmexams
For further information please contact Sorcha directly
