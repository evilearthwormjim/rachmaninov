Students are encouraged to attend every lesson in the term and to practice the required amount for their level to achieve a high standard of the instrument.

Recitals are held at the end of each term for the students. This is very important for gaining performance experience and building confidence.
I emphasise a fun, relaxed atmospere at the recitals as this gives the student a goal to work towards out of exam preparation.

If a student misses a lesson, he/she must inform me at least 24 hours before the class time and we can reschedule.
